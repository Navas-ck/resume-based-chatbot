Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. Born on June 24, 1987, he began his career at FC Barcelona, where he spent over 20 years and became the club’s all-time top scorer. Messi is known for his extraordinary dribbling, vision, passing, and goal-scoring ability.

He has won numerous titles, including multiple UEFA Champions League trophies, La Liga titles, and a record number of Ballon d’Or awards. In 2021, he helped Argentina win the Copa América, and in 2022 he led Argentina to victory in the FIFA World Cup, solidifying his legacy as one of football’s greatest icons. He currently plays club football for Inter Miami in Major League Soccer (MLS).
